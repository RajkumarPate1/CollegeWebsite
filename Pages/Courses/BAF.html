Course Details

• To create for the student of University of Mumbai an additional avenue of self-employment and also to benefit Banks, Insurance Companies and Industries by providing them with suitable trained persons in the field of Accounting and Finance

• To prepare students to exploit opportunities, being newly created, in the Accounting and Finance profession.

• To provide adequate basic understanding about Accounting and Finance education among the students.

• To provide adequate exposure to operational environment in the field of Accounting an Finance .

• To inculcate training and practical approach among the students by using modern technologies in the field of Accounting and Finance.

WHY MMK BAF? :-

Finance is becoming an increasingly complex and critical area in the overall management of all types of institutions- business and other –and for the individual as well. The dual major of Accounting and Finance Provides college student with understanding of financial definition , concept, and relationship coupled with knowledge of the major concept and practices of accounting. This unique combination of skills prepares college student for a wide-array of careers in Accounting and Finance position in banking , industry , government ,or not-for-profit agencies and enhances their opportunities for career advancement.
