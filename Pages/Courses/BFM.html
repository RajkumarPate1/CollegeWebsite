Course Details

• To create additional avenue of Self Employment to students and to provide suitable & trained Persons for Financial Service Sector.

• To prepare graduates to compete with the degree holders in Private & Foriegn Universities.

• To make aware about the practical aspects of the theoretical concepts.

• To establish strong co-ordination between the Services Sectors & the Institutions of Higher Education.

The college tries to follow the objectives laid down by Mumbai University, but unique to MMK are its core values, values that are deeply ingrained in its staff and students and define the culture of the program. They serve as guiding principles in all the activities of the course. The college attempts to inculcate these in its learners in such a way, that they not only practice them while they are students of the course, but also use them throughout their lives.

Financial Services & Financial Sectors Providers are important parts of the Financial Markets FII, FDI, MNCs, FBs, Infrastructural Development in the country through the support of the Government & Private Undertaking, Opening up of the markets in the globalised era, increasing importance of the Stock market operations, Trading & Commercial activities in Mumbai & surrounding areas, formation of SEZs, Development of Twin Mega cities, increasing role of L.T. & B.T. parks, Applications of I.T. in Business Operations, BPO to KPO, International Air & Sea Ports, all these have compelled new generations to learn about is wondering to have pratical oriented degree course which will take care of this service sector, covering the areas of study referred above under the name of B.Com (Financial Markets)
