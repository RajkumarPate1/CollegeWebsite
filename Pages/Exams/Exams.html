Exam Rules

STANDARD OF PASSING

A minimum of 35 % (combined terms) in one paper inclusive of projects.

GRACING

Grace marks are to be awarded under the ordinances 0.5042, 0.5043, 0.5044 and 0.5045 and 0.229 (refer University ordinances).

The total grace marks admissible to a candidate will be 1% of the aggregate marks of the examination under 0.5042 and 1% or 10 which ever is less under 0.5043. Under 0.5044, the total grace marks will be 6. Under 0.5045, grace marks will not be awarded but condonation is admissible to the extent of 1% of the aggregate marks of the examination or 10% of the total number of marks of the head of passing in which candidate is failing whichever is less.

One ordinance i.e 0.5042, 0.5043, 0.5044, 0.5045 and not more than one ordinance can be applied in the case of a candidate. However, grace marks under 0.229 are permissible and a candidate can be given combined benefits of 0.229.

» The facility of obtaining photo / zerox copies of assessed and moderated answer books by the examinee shall be only for theory papers and not for projects or practicals.

» The photo / zerox copies shall be supplied on the payment of non-refundable fee of Rs.100/- per answer book which shall have to be paid by the examinees and 50% concession for reserve category examinees.

» The application shall be submitted to the respective colleges within seven days from the declaration of the result.

» The College shall endevour to supply photo copy of answer books within 7 days from the date of receipt of application.

» The photo / zerox copy will only be issued to the applicant against an identity proof.

» On receipt of the photo / zerox copies the applicant shall be the sole custodian of it and under no circumstances be transferred to any third person for any other purposes.
